
## Section 3: Recommended Syntax Highlighting Colors

### Blue
- Variable types (`Poly`, `Str`, `Int`, `Flt`, `Asa`, etc.)
- Conditions (`if ( A > B )::break`)
- Keywords: `end`, `break`
- Operators: `>`, `<`, `=>`, `<=`, `!`, `|`, `*|`, `!&`, `!|`, `&&`, `!&`

### Red
- Functions
- `null` (e.g., `A = Arr[nopoly int]; Arr[1..10] = null`)

### Pink
- Keywords: `if`, `else`, `ifcase`, `imports`, `def`

### Orange
- Numbers
- Variables
- Arrays
